OpenInMap

Open addresses, coordinates, and shared map links in the map app you choose. OpenInMap is built for quick switching between Apple Maps, Google Maps, Waze, Baidu Maps, Amap, and Tencent Maps.

Share Anywhere

Send text, map links, POIs, or coordinates to OpenInMap from Safari and other iOS apps.

Choose Your Map

Open the same place in the map app that fits your region, route, or preference.

Local First

OpenInMap works on your device. It does not require an account, server, or analytics SDK.

Common Setup

  • Install OpenInMap on your iPhone.
  • Enable the Safari extension in iOS Settings if you want to use OpenInMap from Safari.
  • Use the iOS share sheet to send addresses, coordinates, or map links to OpenInMap from other apps.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

OpenInMap is designed as a local utility. We do not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Data Collection

  • OpenInMap does not require an account.
  • OpenInMap does not use a server to process addresses or coordinates.
  • OpenInMap does not include third-party analytics SDKs.
  • OpenInMap does not save address history on our servers.

Location

If you allow location access, OpenInMap uses your current location on device to show coordinates, heading, altitude, and to open your current position in a selected map app. Location data is not uploaded to us.

Shared Content

When you share text, links, addresses, or coordinates to OpenInMap, the app uses that content locally to prepare a map launch URL.
